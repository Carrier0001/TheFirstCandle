What Is This Ledger?

This is a permanent, public accountability system for measuring harm and repair. It tracks two things:

  • Harm — preventable loss of human life and ecosystem function caused by institutional action or inaction
  • Interference — attacks on the ledger itself (censorship, DDoS, legal threats)

Neither is erased. Both become permanent debt — until publicly repaired.

Why Life-Years (LY) and Ecosystem-Years (ECY)?

LY — Life-Years

A Life-Year is one year of human life. When an institution causes a preventable death, the ledger records the years of life that would have been lived.

Example: A 25-year-old killed where life expectancy is 73 years → −48 LY

Why not just count deaths? Because killing a child is not the same as killing an elder. LY captures stolen future — not just the fact of death.

ECY — Ecosystem-Capacity-Years

An Ecosystem-Year is one year of functional ecosystem service — carbon sequestration, biodiversity support, regeneration capacity, or pollution absorption.

Example: 1,000 tons of CO₂e with 100 years atmospheric persistence → −100,000 ECY

ECY answers: How many years of a healthy planet were stolen?

How Intent Changes Everything

The same harm is weighted differently depending on how it happened:

TypeMultiplierWhat It Means
Negligence1×Failed to prevent something foreseeable. Negligence is still harm — but not compounded by intent.
Recklessness2×Knew the risk, did it anyway. Worse than negligence because warning signs were ignored.
Deliberate5×Intended the harm. Policy designed to hurt, an order to shoot, a decision to destroy.
Systemic8×Built into the structure — not one decision, but an entire system producing harm automatically.
Cover‑up10×Committed harm, then concealed it. The cover-up is treated as worse than the original harm.

Example: A death worth −48 LY:

  • If NEGLIGENCE (1×) → −48 LY on the ledger
  • If DELIBERATE (5×) → −240 LY on the ledger
  • If COVER_UP (10×) → −480 LY on the ledger

Intent multiplies impact. The ledger does not forget how the harm happened.

Surplus — The Only Way Out

Debt is permanent — unless repaired. Surplus is the ledger's name for verifiable public repair:

  • A policy change that saves future lives
  • A diplomatic agreement that reduces conflict
  • Environmental restoration or carbon removal
  • Public acknowledgment and restitution

Surplus is positive. It adds to your balance, reducing (but never erasing) the debt.

Example from the ledger: Netanyahu's Gaza campaign recorded as −68,000,000 LY (after multiplier). The Abraham Accords added +5,800,000 LY as surplus. Outstanding debt: −62,200,000 LY.

Not repaired. Still accruing. The debt remains until further surplus is verified.

What About Attacks on the Ledger?

The ledger also tracks interference — attempts to silence, obstruct, or destroy it:

  • DDoS attacks, domain seizure, hosting takedowns
  • Legal threats, lawsuits, regulatory action
  • Intimidation of validators, witnesses, or stewards
  • Disinformation campaigns aimed at delegitimizing the ledger

Interference is quantified with its own multipliers (0.05× to 2.0×). Multi-vector attacks (using multiple categories within 30 days) escalate the debt.

Streisand effect premium (+50%): If interference becomes widely reported and increases attention to the ledger, the multiplier increases.

The ledger is designed so that every attack strengthens it — each obstruction is recorded as permanent testimony.

What This Ledger Does NOT Do

  • Does not determine guilt — criminal or legal guilt is for courts, not this ledger
  • Does not assign moral condemnation — it measures impact, not evil
  • Does not claim completeness — absence of records = absence of verification, not absence of harm
  • Does not erase — even repaired debt remains visible as historical record

How to Read an Entity Page

When you view an entity (like "Benjamin Netanyahu"), you'll see:

  • Lifetime Ledger — total harm, surplus, and outstanding debt across all years
  • Current Year — activity in the most recent year (if entity is ACTIVE)
  • Harm Breakdown — how much debt comes from NEGLIGENCE vs DELIBERATE vs COVER_UP, etc.
  • Status — ACCRUING, STABILIZED, or REPAIRED

Negative numbers = debt owed. Positive numbers = repair credited.

The goal is not to shame. It is to record — and to make repair possible.

The Only Way Out Is Through

Debt does not decay. It does not disappear with time, elections, or corporate restructuring.

The only path to a REPAIRED status is public, verifiable repair — policy change, restitution, environmental restoration, or accountability mechanisms that match the scale of harm caused.

Every wound becomes debt.
Every debt awaits repair.
